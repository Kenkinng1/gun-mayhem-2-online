Gun Mayhem 2 - Online Multiplayer

Play Gun Mayhem 2 with a friend over the internet! One player hosts, the other joins.

Host: Runs the game and streams video to the guest.
Guest: Watches the stream and sends keyboard inputs to the host.

Host: Uses the default in-game controls (arrow keys + L-Shift to shoot, E to throw)

Guest: Customizable! Defaults to arrow keys + L-Shift to shoot, E to throw (mirrors host).
